About Me


I am a Research Engineer at Siemens Corporate Technology, Bengaluru. I have completed my Ph.D. from Department of Computer Science and Automation, Indian Institute of Science, Bengaluru, under Dr. K V Raghavan. My research interests are in the fields of programming languages, program analysis, and program verification. I am excited by theoretically challenging problems that require creative engineering efforts. I love developing useful tools for programmers that enhance program comprehension, reliability and efficiency, and can keep up with the ever-evolving software and systems domain.
